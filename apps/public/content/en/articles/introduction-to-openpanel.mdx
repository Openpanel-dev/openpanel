OpenPanel is an open-source analytics platform — an alternative to Mixpanel and Google Analytics that keeps you in control of your data.

## Why open source?

Our code is public. Anyone can read it, contribute, or check exactly how their data is handled. You can explore the code and contribute on [GitHub](https://git.new/openpanel).

## Why choose OpenPanel?

We started OpenPanel as an open-source alternative to Mixpanel. Mixpanel is strong at product analytics but doesn't cover [web analytics](/features/web-analytics) well, so we built OpenPanel to do both in one tool.

For a detailed comparison with other tools, see our guide on [open source web analytics](/articles/self-hosted-web-analytics).

## What can you do with OpenPanel?

OpenPanel includes:

-   **Web Analytics**: Insights similar to Plausible, Fathom, and Simple Analytics.
-   **Product Analytics**: Track product usage and user interactions, like Mixpanel.
-   **[User Retention](/features/retention)**: Track and improve how users come back.
-   **[Funnels](/features/funnels)**: See user journeys and [conversion paths](/features/conversion).
-   **Events**: Track specific user actions.
-   **Profiles**: Build per-user profiles to understand your audience.
-   **Real-Time View**: Put live data on a big monitor in the office.
-   **Export API**: Export your raw data via the API.
-   **Chart API**: Pull chart data into your own dashboards.

## Commitment to privacy

Your data is your property and you control it. The tracking script is open-source and complies with GDPR and CCPA. We don't use cookies — instead we use a privacy-friendly hashed identifier that rotates daily.

## Your data is safe with us

We never sell your data. We charge a fee to run the cloud service, or you can self-host OpenPanel for free and keep everything on your own infrastructure. Export or delete your data anytime, with no lock-in.

## Listening to feedback

Feedback shapes what we build. Tell us what's missing and we'll work on it.
